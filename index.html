ABOUT V. SOMANNA

Since 1983, Shri V. Somanna has dedicated his passion, time, and efforts to social service, political wellness & infrastructural development. His hard work & service-mindedness have catapulted him as one of the most eminent leaders in the state of Karnataka.

Shri V. Somanna followed his interests, aspirations, and stature in social service and politics. Since then, Shri V Somanna has held many parliamentary positions – Twice as BBMP Corporator, 5 times as MLA, 2 times as MLC, and 5 times as Minister holding portfolios such as Bangalore Development, Food and Civil Supplies, Housing, Horticulture, Sericulture, Muzrai, Prisons, Home Guards & Sainik Welfare.

Organised ‘Gorakshana Abhiyana’ at Hampinagar, Bangalore under the guidance of Sri. Ragaveshwara Bharati Swamji pontiff of Ramachandrapura Mutt. Organized free medical camps for the poor and distributed books for the poor students. Honoured several people who served in various fields.

Distributed more than 50,000 Food kits, Sanitizers, Gloves, Medical kits, Masks to the poorer class of the society and also distributed PPE kit and Oxygen Cylinders to Hospitals during Covid pandemic

With an intention to make Dasara celebrations reach the rural masses, Taluk level Raitha Dasara/Grameena Dasara celebrations were widely encouraged by conducting several sports activities. In order to ensure successful celebration of 9 day Navarathri festival, various Dasara Sub-committees were constituted to oversee various cultural programmes.
