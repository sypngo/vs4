Achievement

JOURNEY


  • 1983 - 1994

    Served as an Honorable Corporator, BBMP (Bengaluru) elected by the public 2 times continuously

  • 1994 - 1999

    MLA, Binnipete Assembly Constituency, Minister For Prisons & Minister For Bengaluru Development

  • 1999 – 2004

    Won a landslide victory again as MLA, Binnipete Assembly Constituency Bengaluru (Independent Candidate)

  • 2004 - 2007

    Elected again for the second time as MLA, Binnipete Assembly Constituency (Bengaluru)

  • 2008 – 2009

    Moved to Govindrajanagara Assembly Constituency and got elected as MLA for the period of one year

  • 2010 – 2016

    Elected as MLC from BJP and was made Minister Of Food / Civil Supplies & Minister Of Housing and Muzrai

  • 2016 – 2018

    Elected again for the second consecutive time as the MLC from BJP cabinet

  • 2018 – Present

    Elected As MLA For Govindrajanagara Assembly Constituency & Housing & Infrastructure Minister For Bengaluru

  • 2019 – 2020

    Selected as the minister for Horticulture & Sericulture for period of 1 year
